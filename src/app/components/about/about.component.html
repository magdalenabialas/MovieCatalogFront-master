The use of recommendation mechanisms on the example of an application used to recommend movies.

University: The Silesian University of Technology Gliwice

Field of study: Computer Science

Author: Magdalena Galwa

The goal of the project is to develop an application which allows users to browse movie data and rate those movies.

Users rates related to their personal profiles are the basis of recommendations (another movies worth to watch / rate) prepared by recommendation engine and visible for the user.
